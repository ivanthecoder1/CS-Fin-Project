Mental Health advice

While it is important for people of color to learn about programming and finance, it is of utmost significance to care for our mental health. People belonging to racially marginalized communities are more likely, on average, to suffer from poor mental health due to discrimination. Our mission is to provide POC with accessible educational resources, specifically tailored towards their unique experiences.
